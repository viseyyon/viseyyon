Our Story

Viseyyon was born from a simple observation: the teams managing 100+ million connected devices needed better tools. Traditional DevOps approaches couldn't keep up with the complexity of modern embedded systems, IoT devices, and global software deployments.

We started by building internal tools for enterprise device manufacturers - root cause analysis systems, CI/CD optimizers, and security platforms that could handle the scale and complexity of real-world deployments.

Today, we're making these enterprise-proven tools available to everyone. Our portfolio of 32+ AI-powered products serves customers from solo developers to global enterprises, all built on the same robust architecture that manages billions of daily events.
