Company Overview

Viseyyon Technologies is a leading provider of AI-powered enterprise solutions, specializing in DevOps automation, root cause analysis, and security tools. Founded with a mission to democratize enterprise AI, Viseyyon serves customers from individual developers to Fortune 500 companies.

The company's flagship product, NEXUS-RCA, pioneered the use of multi-LLM consensus engines for root cause analysis, achieving 94%+ accuracy by combining five leading AI models. Viseyyon's technology manages over 100 million connected devices and processes billions of events daily.

Headquartered in Bangalore, India, with a fully remote team spanning 15 countries, Viseyyon is backed by leading enterprise software investors and has grown to serve hundreds of enterprise customers worldwide.
